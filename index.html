Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process

What services do you offer?

I offer comprehensive web development services including frontend development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Flutter. I also provide backend development using Python, Java, PHP, and various databases like MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB. Additionally, I work with modern frameworks like Django and SpringBoot.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and requirements. A simple website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex web applications can take 6-12 weeks. I always provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the development process.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, I offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages. This includes bug fixes, security updates, content updates, performance optimization, and feature enhancements. I believe in building long-term relationships with my clients.

What is your development process?

My development process includes: 1) Initial consultation and requirements gathering, 2) Planning and wireframing, 3) Design mockups and approval, 4) Development with regular updates, 5) Testing and quality assurance, 6) Deployment and launch, 7) Post-launch support and training.

Can you work with my existing website?

Absolutely! I can work with existing websites to add new features, improve performance, enhance design, or completely redesign them. I'm experienced with various CMS platforms including WordPress and can work with custom-built solutions as well.

What are your rates and payment terms?

My rates are competitive and based on project scope and complexity. I offer both fixed-price projects and hourly rates. Payment terms typically include a 50% upfront payment to begin work, with the remainder due upon completion. I accept various payment methods and provide detailed invoices.
